## The structure of operating system

### Hierarchy

* Advantage
  1. Convenient for the OS to debug and valid, simplify the
  design and implementation of the system.
  2. Easy to extend and maintain.
* Disadvantage
  1. Low efficiency
  2. Difficult to define the layer reasonably.

### Modularization

Modularization means splitting the operating system into some independent
modules by functions.
